Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how "Turko" ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). 
Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your information as described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.
---
## 1. Information We Collect
We may collect information about you in a variety of ways depending on how you interact with the App.
### A. Personal Data
While using our App, we may collect personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you, especially if you create an account:
*   **Account Information:** Email address, username, password, and authentication details (e.g., if you sign in using Firebase Authentication or Apple Sign-In).
*   **Profile Information:** Profile picture or other display preferences you set.
### B. Audio and Microphone Data
*   **Microphone Access:** If you use speech recognition, dialog practices, or audio recording features to practice Turkish pronunciation, the App will request permission to access your device’s microphone.
*   **Voice Recordings:** Depending on the feature implementation, voice input may be processed locally on your device (using iOS native speech-to-text engines) or transmitted securely to speech recognition services to provide feedback. We do not store your raw audio files on our servers unless explicitly stated and agreed upon.
### C. Usage and Device Data
We automatically collect certain information when you access and use the App:
*   **Progress Tracking:** Learning progress, completed lessons, mistake history, dialogue transcripts, and performance statistics.
*   **Device Information:** Mobile device ID, model, manufacturer, operating system version, and system configuration.
*   **Crash & Performance Reports:** Diagnostics and crash reports collected via Firebase Crashlytics to help us debug issues and improve application stability.
*   **Analytics Data:** App interaction details, feature usage patterns, and session durations (processed via Google Analytics/Firebase Analytics).
